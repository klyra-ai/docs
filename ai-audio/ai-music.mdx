## How to Use

> üîé **Navigation:** Dashboard ‚Üí Left Panel ‚Üí AI Audio & Voice ‚Üí AI Music

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Model">
    You can choose between:

    - **Stable Audio** ‚Üí Best for instrumentals, soundscapes, and musical structures.
    - **Minimax Music** ‚Üí Best for songs with vocals, genre-rich compositions, and style-matching from reference tracks.

    > ‚ö° Settings and options change dynamically based on your selection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Using Stable Audio">
    1. Enter a **Title** for your music generation task.
    2. Write your **Prompt** ‚Äî be as descriptive as possible (e.g., _‚ÄúA powerful orchestral soundtrack with soaring strings, deep percussion, and a heroic brass section. Starts softly with piano, builds into an epic climax with drums and horns, ending with a calm fade.‚Äù_).
    3. Ensure **Stable Audio** is selected.
    4. Set **Total Seconds** ‚Üí Maximum is **47 seconds**.
    5. Set **Steps** (for denoising/refinement) or leave default. ‚Üí Maximum is **1000**.
    6. Click **Generate**.

    **Key Features of Stable Audio:**

    - Generates at **44.1 kHz stereo**.
    - Produces **intro, development, and outro** with coherent structure.
    - Great for **melodies, background music, and sound effects**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Using Minimax Music">
    1. Enter a **Title** for your music generation task.
    2. **Enter your Prompt or Lyrics** ‚Äî describe the style, mood, or write short lyrics. If you‚Äôre adding lyrics, wrap them in `##` at the beginning and end, like this:
       - (e.g., _‚Äú##Dancing lights and hearts collide,_

         _We move together, side by side._

         _Through the night our spirits fly,_

         _Hand in hand, just you and I.##‚Äù_).
    3. Ensure **Minimax Music** is selected.
    4. Upload a **Reference Audio** file to guide the style and feel of your music. Make sure the clip is at least 15 seconds long.
    5. Click **Generate**.

    **Key Features of Minimax Music:**

    - Simultaneous **accompaniment \+ vocals**.
    - Upload reference tracks to **mimic style**.
    - Converts **lyrics into music**.
    - Supports multiple genres (pop, rock, classical, electronic, etc.).
    - Maximum output length = **60 seconds**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Your Results">
    - Once generated, your music will appear under **Results**.
    - You can **Play, Download, or Delete** the tracks.
    - All results are also saved under **AI Panel ‚Üí Documents ‚Üí All Voiceovers (audio category)** for future access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### üí° Tips

- **Be descriptive** in your prompts ‚Äî include genre, instruments, mood, and tempo.
- **Use Stable Audio** for instrumental or cinematic music.
- **Use Minimax Music** when you want full songs with vocals.
- **Experiment with reference tracks** in Minimax to achieve stylistic accuracy.
- **Keep duration in mind** ‚Äî 47s (Stable Audio) vs. 60s (Minimax).
- **Save and organize** results into workbooks for specific projects (podcasts, campaigns, etc.).
