## Introduction

The **AI Social Media Suite** in Klyra AI streamlines your content publishing process. Instead of juggling multiple platforms, you can:

- **Create posts manually** or generate them with AI assistance.
- **Preview posts live** before publishing.
- **Upload media assets** directly into your post.
- **Connect and manage accounts** across X, LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook.
- **Schedule posts** to go out immediately, at a future date/time, or even as recurring reposts.
- **Track performance** through a unified **Posts Calendar** and **Scheduled Posts view**.

This tool helps creators, marketers, and businesses **orchestrate their social media strategy from one central hub.**

<Warning>
  Access to this feature is only available with a **Starter** or **Premium** subscription plan.
</Warning>
