Go to `Account` → `Subscription Plans` tab in the left panel.

## Subscription Overview

At the top of this page, you can view your active subscription details, billing schedule, and remaining usage credits.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan details">
    - **Current Plan:** Displays the active subscription plan (e.g., Premium, Starter, Basic, Free).
    - **Billing Type:** Indicates whether it is a monthly or yearly subscription.
    - **Cost:** Shows the recurring price for your current plan.
    - **Next Billing Date:** Displays the exact date and time of your next renewal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account actions">
    Beneath the plan details, you will see quick-action links:

    - **View Orders:** Opens your full billing and order history.
    - **Cancel Subscription:** Initiates the cancellation process for your plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remaining credits">
    Your usage counters reset at the start of each new billing cycle.

    - **Word Credits Left:** The remaining text generation credits.
    - **Media Credits Left:** The remaining image/video generation credits.
    - **Characters Left:** The remaining character count for voiceover or transcription tasks.
    - **Minutes Left:** The remaining audio or video processing minutes.

    <Tip>
      Keep an eye on your available credits to avoid interruptions during your projects.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

### Upgrading or Downgrading

If you need more credits, access to additional features, or want to reduce your subscription level:

1. Review the plan comparison above.
2. Select the desired plan.
3. Confirm your choice — any changes will be reflected immediately.

<Check>
  Consider switching to a yearly plan to save up to **20%** compared to monthly pricing.
</Check>
