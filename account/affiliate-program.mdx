**Navigation:** Go to `Account` → `Affiliate Program` in the left panel of the dashboard.

## Overview

At the top of the page, you’ll find your affiliate program summary:

- **Referral Link** – Your unique tracking link to share with friends, colleagues, or your audience.
- **Total Referred Users** – Displays the number of people who signed up through your link.
- **Earnings** – Shows your total accumulated earnings to date.
- **Referral Commission Rate** – The percentage of your referred user’s **first purchase** you earn as commission.
- **Referral Program Type** – Indicates that commissions are earned from **first purchases only**.

## How it works

The referral process is simple and consists of three steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    Share your referral link via email, social media, or messaging apps, and tell your friends about the benefits of using the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Registration">
    Your friends must register using the referral link you provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Commissions">
    Once they make their first subscription plan payment, you earn a commission.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Focus on inviting users who are likely to subscribe to a paid plan to maximize your commission earnings.
</Tip>

## Payout request

Once you’ve accumulated at least **\$10**, you can request a payout.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Payment Method">
    Choose your payment method – Either PayPal or Bank Transfer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payout details:">
    - **For PayPal:** Enter your PayPal ID.
    - **For Bank Transfer:** Fill in all required fields:
      - Bank Name
      - Account Name / Full Name
      - Account Number / IBAN
      - BIC / SWIFT
      - Routing Number (if applicable)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request Amount">
    **Enter the requested amount** – Minimum \$10.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request">
    Click the **Request** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Ensure all payout information is correct to avoid delays or failed transfers.
</Warning>

### Invite your friends

You can also send invitations directly from the dashboard:

1. Enter your friend’s **email address** in the provided field.
2. Click the **Invite** button.
3. An invitation email with your referral link will be sent automatically.

### My payout requests (all time)

This table lists all your payout requests, showing:

- Requested Date
- Request ID
- Total Amount (USD)
- Preferred Payment Gateway
- Status (e.g., Pending, Approved, Paid)
- Actions (view or cancel request)

### Earned commissions (all time)

Here you can see the details of all commissions earned from your referrals:

- Purchase Date
- Order ID
- Total Payment (USD) made by your referral
- Commission Rate
- Earned Commissions (USD)
