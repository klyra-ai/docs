### 1. Personal Details

View your current profile information including:

- Full Name
- Email
- Referral ID & Referral Link
- Job Role
- Company
- Website
- City & Country
- Phone Number

### 2. Update Profile

Edit your personal and company information:

- **Edit Profile:** Full Name, Job Role, Email Address, Phone Number
- **Change Avatar** by uploading a new profile image
- **Company Information:** Company Name, Address Line, City, Postal Code, Country

Click **Save Changes** after updating.

### 3. Set Defaults

Configure your preferred default settings:

- Default AI Voiceover Studio Language
- Default AI Voiceover Studio Voice
- Default Language for Templates
- Default AI Model for Templates
- Default AI Model for Chatbots
- Default AI Image Model

These settings will be automatically applied when you use the respective tools.

### 4. Change Password

- Enter your **Current Password**
- Enter your **New Password**
- Confirm your **New Password**
- Click **Change** to update

### 5. 2FA Authentication

Enhance your account security by enabling **Google Two-Factor Authentication**.

1. Click **Activate 2FA Authentication**
2. Scan the QR code shown on the page **or** use the provided **Setup Key** in your Google Authenticator app
3. Open the Google Authenticator app on your device (install it from your mobile app store if not already installed)
4. Enter the **6-digit OTP** generated by the app
5. Click **Activate**

<Tip>
  Google Authenticator generates time-based codes required during the login process for added security.
</Tip>

### 6. Delete Account

<Warning>
  This action is irreversible.\
  Deleting your account will remove all your data and generated results. You will not be able to recover them.
</Warning>

**Steps:**

1. Check the box **I confirm that I would like to completely delete my account on this platform**
2. Click **Yes, Delete My Account**
