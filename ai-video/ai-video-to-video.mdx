## Introduction

The **AI Video to Video** feature lets you take an original video and reimagine it with advanced AI-driven animation and transformation. Whether you want to:

- Add **new artistic styles**,
- Enhance motion for **better consistency**, or
- Upscale videos for **higher resolution**,

Klyra AI provides the tools you need to **breathe new life into your video assets**.

Powered by cutting-edge AI models, you can achieve professional video re-animation with just a few clicks:

- **CogVideoX 5b**
- **AnimateDiff**
- **Fast AnimateDiff**
- **Upscale Video**

<Warning>
  Access to this feature is only available with a **Starter** or **Premium** subscription plan.
</Warning>

## How to Use

> 🔎 **Navigation:** Dashboard → Left Panel → AI Video Creation → AI Video to Video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Task">
    - Go to the **AI Video to Video** tab.
    - (Optional) Enter a **Video Title** for project tracking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Target Video">
    - Upload the video you want to re-animate.
    - Supported formats: **.mp4, .mov, .webm, .m4v, .gif**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Model">
    - Choose from:
      - **CogVideoX 5b** → Advanced re-animation with smooth motion.
      - **AnimateDiff** → Detailed, artistic transformations.
      - **Fast AnimateDiff** → Faster processing, great for quick previews.
      - **Upscale Video** → Enhance resolution and quality.
    - Once selected, **settings will update dynamically** depending on the chosen model.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Video Settings">
    - Enter a **Prompt** → Describe the desired transformation (e.g., _“Make this video look like a watercolor animation with smooth transitions”_).
    - Adjust other **model-specific settings** (if available). Defaults can be left unchanged for most cases.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Video">
    - Click **Generate**.
    - Wait while the AI processes and transforms your video.
    - The result will appear in the **Results section**, where you can **Play, Download, or Delete** it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 💡 Tips

- **Match the model to your goal**:
  - _CogVideoX 5b_ → Smooth animation, cinematic consistency.
  - _AnimateDiff_ → Artistic, experimental results.
  - _Fast AnimateDiff_ → Quick iterations and testing.
  - _Upscale Video_ → Sharpen and enhance existing videos.
- **Use descriptive prompts** → Mention styles (cartoon, cinematic, surreal), colors, or effects.
- **Start with short videos** → Shorter clips (5–15s) help preview results faster.
- **Upscale final outputs** → For professional projects, run the result through **Upscale Video** for added sharpness.
- **Experiment with defaults** → Many models produce great results without tweaking settings.
